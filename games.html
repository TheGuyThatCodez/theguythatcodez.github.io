MY GAMES

AREA:001 is a Rooms-inspired game with a focus on replayability, with 3 300-room long sections. Each section has its own entities and gimmicks. Can you get to the end, or will you let yourself be your own stopping point?

Acid Cat is a 2d platformer inspired by Will You Snail? and Celeste. It is about a cat... that spits acid. Along with an in-depth level editor, there are many secrets and challenges hidden through-out the levels.

FRAGMENTED is a rougelike PvE game where you fight against a crystal-zombie virus. With many weapons, tools, and upgrades to choose from, eliminate the hoard with your friends however you'd like.

GAMES I WORK ON

BREACHED is a SCP-themed asymmetrical horror game on roblox. Play as an Anomaly and wipe out everybody else, or play as a Personnel and survive until time runs out.

ROOMS: REVISITED is a Rooms-like inspired by DOORS, with many badges to collect and entities to encounter. Will you step into the office that just keeps on giving?
